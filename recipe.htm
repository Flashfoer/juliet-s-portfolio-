Recipe 1: Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and milk together in a bowl.
  2. Add the vegetable oil and mix well.
  3. Pour the mixture into a greased baking pan.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Recipe 2:Mexican Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups rice
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 onion,diced
  • 1 garlic clove,crushed
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 Roma tomatoes
  • 2 cups of hot water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 sprigs cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.Drain and set aside
  2. Heat the olive oil over medium heat in a large saucepan. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the crushed garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
  4. Stir in the chicken bouillon, garlic powder, and salt. Cook for 1-2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Add the Roma tomatoes, breaking them up with a spoon, and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  6. Add the rinsed rice, stirring to coat it with the oil and seasoning mixture. Cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the rice to toast slightly.
  7. Pour in the hot water, stirring to combine. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the saucepan, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the cilantro sprigs. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
